hum baed almawti Definition of ongoing charity

Ongoing charity is charity whose reward continues even after death, which is what many people strive for by establishing an ongoing charity project so that it will intercede for them with God, be in the balance of their good deeds, and its reward will continue for them after death.

Ideas for ongoing charity

Watering water Giving water is considered one of God's most beloved types of ongoing charity, and it is the best charity that the Messenger of God - may God bless him and grant him peace - urged us to do.On the authority of Saad bin Ubadah, he said: I said: O Messenger of God, which charity is better? He said: (Irrigating water) [Ibn Majah] Irrigating water is considered the greatest investment a believer can make, as its reward lasts until after death. Water is the lifeblood and its most important resource, and irrigating water is a way to forgive sins and sins, no matter how great they are, and we must pay attention to this type of charity because of its great virtue and great reward with God Almighty. .

What are the types of ongoing charity?

There is no specific type of ongoing charity. Everything that has a continuous, beneficial and useful effect for people is considered ongoing charity. However, there are many doors that people knock on to get closer to God through ongoing charity, including: Planting trees, establishing water connections, building mosques, or printing the Holy Qur’an and distributing it seeking God’s pleasure.
