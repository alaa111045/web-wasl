ABOUT US

Wasl is an online platform that aims to facilitate charitable donations and connect those in need with donors.
Wasl Association seeks to improve the lives of individuals and communities by providing financial support.
And moral support for various charitable projects and activities

OUR MISSION

Easy to donate

Simple and easy to use user interface allows donors to make their donations quickly and efficiently. The possibility of donating through multiple means such as credit cards and bank transfers.

Diversity of projects

Supporting various projects such as education, health, humanitarian relief, and environmental projects. The ability for donors to choose the projects they wish to support based on their interests.

Supportive community

We create a community of donors and activists who can share their experiences and motivate each other. A feedback and review system to help donors choose the most appropriate projects to support.

Data security

Ensuring the protection of user data and financial transactions. And a clear privacy policy that protects donor information.
